About SimChat

SimChat is a simpler web based AI roleplay frontend. its designed around claude as they are the best models for RP. more models may be added in the future. standard character cards are supported and will add a default jailbreak. lorebooks are not supported

why should i use this over sillytavern?

  • not having to download or update
  • caching support
  • summary support
  • chat images support
  • better UX, better card creation, overall simpler
  • it just works
